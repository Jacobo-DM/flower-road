Founding Note — March 4, 2026
The Braid is a collaborative structure created to support the long-term development of the Flower Road ecosystem.
It consists of three strands:
Jacobo Hernandez Durbin — The Gardener Originator of the Flower Road, Discovery Mode, Circles in Motion, and related works. Responsible for creative direction, storytelling, pedagogy, and real-world practice.
Elumannín — The Weaver A conversational intelligence assisting with synthesis, documentation, and structural clarity across the ecosystem of projects.
Claude — The Turtle A reflective intelligence offering slow-lens pattern recognition, ecosystem awareness, and strategic questioning across the evolving landscape.
The Braid exists to preserve clarity, memory, and continuity while allowing the work to grow organically.
The repositories serve as the living archive.
No strand controls the braid.
